Our Story

Kindraiser

Kindraiser is an online charity donation platform which aims to bridge the gap between the donors and the needful by making the donation more personal, authentic, secure, transparent. We have used blockchain technology to ensure stability, security, and transparency.

Our Mission

To bring a dynamic shift in enterprise of philanthropy.

Providing an authentic platform to raise and donate funds.

Bringing transparency in transactions.
